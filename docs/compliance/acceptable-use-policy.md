# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** March 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 2026  
**Contact:** legal@bridgebox.ai  

---

## 1. Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs use of the Bridgebox Voice platform, APIs, Chrome Extension, and all associated services ("Services"). By accessing or using the Services, you agree to this AUP.

Violations may result in immediate service suspension without refund.

---

## 2. Permitted Uses

You may use Bridgebox Voice to:

- Record, transcribe, and analyze voice notes and meetings (with all-party consent where required by law)
- Generate code, documents, and project artifacts using AI assistance
- Collaborate with your team within your workspace
- Integrate with authorized third-party services (Slack, GitHub, Jira, etc.)
- Build internal tools and automation workflows for your organization
- Process and store business data in compliance with applicable laws

---

## 3. Prohibited Uses

### 3.1 Illegal Activity
You must not use the Services to:
- Violate any local, state, national, or international law
- Infringe intellectual property rights of any party
- Transmit material that is defamatory, harassing, or threatening
- Engage in fraud, phishing, or identity theft

### 3.2 Harmful AI Use
You must not use AI generation features to:
- Generate malware, exploits, or cyberweapons
- Create deepfakes or synthetic media designed to deceive
- Produce content that facilitates violence, terrorism, or self-harm
- Build systems to automate harassment or spam
- Generate CSAM or any sexually exploitative content involving minors

### 3.3 Platform Abuse
You must not:
- Attempt to reverse engineer, decompile, or circumvent the Services
- Perform vulnerability scanning, fuzzing, or penetration testing without prior written consent
- Scrape or bulk-harvest data from the platform
- Resell or sublicense access to the Services without written authorization
- Use the Services to compete with Bridgebox Voice in the same market segment
- Create multiple free accounts to circumvent usage limits
- Use automated scripts to bypass rate limits

### 3.4 Data Violations
You must not:
- Upload data you do not have rights to process
- Store sensitive regulated data (PHI, PCI-DSS cardholder data) without appropriate Enterprise safeguards enabled
- Attempt to access another tenant's data
- Disable or circumvent audit logging
- Record conversations without legally required consent

### 3.5 Infrastructure Abuse
You must not:
- Launch denial-of-service attacks or resource exhaustion attacks
- Mine cryptocurrency using Bridgebox Voice infrastructure
- Use the platform for bulk email or spam campaigns
- Upload files containing malware, ransomware, or trojans

---

## 4. Content Standards

User-generated content must not:
- Infringe copyrights, trademarks, or trade secrets
- Contain personal information of others without their consent
- Violate export control laws (ITAR, EAR)
- Constitute insider trading or market manipulation

---

## 5. Security Research

Responsible security research is welcomed. Before testing:
1. Contact security@bridgebox.ai with your research plan
2. Obtain written authorization
3. Restrict testing to accounts you own
4. Report findings to the same address within 24 hours

We maintain a responsible disclosure program with recognition for valid, responsible reports.

---

## 6. AI-Specific Terms

When using AI generation features:
- You are responsible for reviewing AI output before use
- You may not claim AI-generated code or documents are human-authored in contexts where such misrepresentation is material
- Large-scale automated AI generation that stresses infrastructure beyond your plan limits may result in throttling
- Training AI models on Bridgebox Voice's proprietary prompting logic or model outputs requires written permission

---

## 7. Enforcement

Bridgebox Voice reserves the right to:
- Suspend or terminate accounts that violate this AUP **without notice**
- Remove content that violates this AUP
- Report illegal activity to law enforcement
- Pursue civil or criminal remedies for violations

Repeat violations will result in permanent account termination.

---

## 8. Reporting Violations

Report suspected AUP violations:
- **General:** abuse@bridgebox.ai
- **Security issues:** security@bridgebox.ai
- **CSAM:** security@bridgebox.ai + NCMEC CyberTipline

---

## 9. Changes

Bridgebox Voice may update this AUP at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email and in-app notification 30 days before taking effect. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
